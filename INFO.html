TAMAN RIMBA RIANG

 This recreational park is use by many people where they could go and have a break with their family or friends. Where they can breath a fresh air and have fun as well as make memories with their family or friends by spending their time together at the park. For children, they can play in the playground and explore more of the outside world with their parents and even learn new things to expend their knowledge about it.
